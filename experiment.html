Despite being in the HTML specification since a long time, developers could not make use of it until recently. Explain why that might be the case (Hint: Check the caniuse.com page for MathML!) (5 points)

Firefox has had native MathML support for years, but Chrome, Edge, and Safari had little or no support. Google Chrome (the most widely used browser) only started improving MathML support in 2023. This means MathML elements were often rendered differently depending on the browser, making it unreliable for web applications.
